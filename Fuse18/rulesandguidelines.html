Fuse Plots Rules and Guidelines

  1. Follow all rules put in place by the Palace Network. (Found Here: https://forums.palace.network/forum/4-community-information/)
  2. Listen to, and respect all Fuse Staff members while visiting our plots.
  3. On attractions, unless otherwise stated, do not press W or SHIFT and stay seated for the entire attraction.
  4. Remain in guest areas, on pathways and avoid going backstage unless specific permission has been given. This means no flying or parkour.
  5. Follow all signage that has been put in place on our plots.
  6. Failure to comply with these rules may or may not result in a ban from Fuse plots.

IMPORTANT NOTE - Please note that these guidelines only reflect the guidelines for Fuse Entertainment and are only valid when visiting a Fuse Entertainment Plot. Other plots may have guidelines that differ from our guidelines. You should check with the plot owner when considering a plots guidelines or rules. Thank you for your cooperation!

-Fuse Entertainment Staff Team
