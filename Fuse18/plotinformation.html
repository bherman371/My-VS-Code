EpiCentral (50;87): The Center for all things Fuse. Home to Fuse HQ, Spark Convention Center, The Web Theatre, and Tempo Landing Resort.

Fuse's Hollywood Adventure (Opening Soon) Shout “Hooray for Hollywood!” in this classic, yet modern park of the Hollywood that never was and always will be.

Fuse Resort Main Parking (6;-27): The main parking area for Fuse Resort Palace. Direct tram access to Fantasy Valley with bus and watercraft access to EpiCentral and all other Fuse plots.
